Jolan Chappaz — Tattoo Artist, Graphic Artist & Poet

Jolan Chappaz is a Swiss hand-poked tattoo artist, graphic artist and poet. He works at Nuit Noire Tattoo in Geneva and from his home atelier in Villars-sur-Ollon, Switzerland. His work spans tattoos, paintings, poetry and self-published artist books, drawing inspiration from Swiss folk art and the history of tattooing.

  • Tattoos — hand-poked, machine-free tattooing inspired by Swiss folk art.
  • Paintings — acrylic works on cardboard.
  • Bonne Nuit (2025) — a poetry and flash artist book, limited edition.
  • À la fin (2024) — an artistic dialogue with Cécile Giovannini.
  • Merch — screen-printed bandanas and prints.
  • About — biography and contact.
